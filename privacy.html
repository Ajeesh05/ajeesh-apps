Privacy Policy

At Ajeesh Apps, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines what data we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have regarding your information when using our web apps and Chrome extensions.

Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum amount of data necessary to provide and improve our services. This may include:

  • Usage data to diagnose technical issues and enhance user experience.
  • Anonymous analytics data to understand how our apps are used.
  • Contact information only when you choose to reach out to us via email or feedback forms.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to improve functionality, enhance security, and communicate important updates. We never sell or rent your data to third parties. Your data helps us:

  • Maintain and improve app performance.
  • Personalize user experience and troubleshoot issues.
  • Respond to your inquiries and feedback.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure. While we strive to safeguard your information, no transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to support our operations (e.g., hosting, analytics). These services are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only as instructed.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
